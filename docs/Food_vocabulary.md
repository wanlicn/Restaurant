# 烹饪食物 词汇对照表- Culinary Vocabulary

### Terms
- Heat & Cooking Skills  火候 & 烹饪技法
  - low heat 小火 // a small fire
  - turn the heat on low 开小火
  - turn the heat to the lowest setting 火开到最小档
  - Medium high heat 中大火
  - High heat 大火 
  - turn to lower heat 转小火
  - turn up to heat 转大火

  - boil over low heat 小火煮

  - season with salt 加点盐调味

### Preparation  预制处理
- Water soak first then wash clean 水先浸泡然后清洗
- soak in the water to prevent oxidation 浸泡在水中防止氧化
  - water washed off the excess starch on potatoes 清水洗去土豆表明多余的淀粉
  - Bell peppers are bringing vibrant colors 菜椒可以增加鲜艳的色彩
  - Add a bit sugar to balance the sour taste from the vinegar 加一点白糖中和一下醋的酸味
- drain 
- Marinate over night in the fridge 冷藏室隔夜腌制
- store in the freezer 冷冻保存
- chill them in fridge before serving
- Salt a pinch 盐一点点，一小撮盐，一小把。。。
- drain 沥干
- When the chili turn color into darker remove them with strainer 辣椒变色时用滤网捞走辣椒
- frying the chili seed, the simple hotness from chili is transferred into a kind of mild hotness with fragrant 辣椒籽经过煎制，原来单一的辣味变成温和的香辣味
- there is no extra sauce left on the plate since the flavor is coated on each single stripe 盘中没有多余的汁水，所有的汁水都附着在土豆丝的表面
- defrost 解冻  defrost for 1'30" in a microwave oven 用微波炉解冻
